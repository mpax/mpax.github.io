WireSmith

Electrical Calculator

A fast, professional electrical calculator designed for electricians, engineers, technicians, and students. Perform common electrical calculations directly on your phone with a clean interface built for real jobsite use. All calculations run offline and update instantly as values change.

  • Wire ampacity calculator
  • Voltage drop calculator
  • Conduit fill calculator
  • Motor full load current (FLC) reference
  • Electrical math utilities
  • Calculation history with exportable reports
Currently available on iOS. Android version coming soon.
