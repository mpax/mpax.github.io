Privacy Policy

WireSmith

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

WireSmith respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the WireSmith mobile application.

1. Overview

WireSmith is designed to be a local calculation tool. Most features operate entirely on your device and do not require personal information. We aim to collect the minimum amount of data necessary to operate and improve the application.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

WireSmith does not collect or store personal information, including:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Financial information

The app can be used without creating an account.

3. Local Data Storage

WireSmith may store certain information locally on your device to improve usability. This may include:

  • Calculation history
  • Recently used inputs
  • App preferences

This data:

  • Stays on your device
  • Is not transmitted to external servers
  • Can be removed by deleting the app

4. Analytics (Optional)

To improve the performance and usability of WireSmith, the app may use anonymous analytics tools. These analytics may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Feature usage frequency
  • Crash reports

This information:

  • Does not identify individual users
  • Is used only to improve the application

If analytics are used, they may be provided by services such as:

  • Google (Firebase Analytics)
  • Apple (App Analytics)

Users can disable analytics through device privacy settings where available.

5. Third-Party Services

WireSmith may rely on third-party services to support the application, such as:

  • Crash reporting tools
  • Analytics platforms
  • App distribution platforms

These services operate under their own privacy policies. WireSmith does not sell or share user data for advertising purposes.

6. Data Security

WireSmith does not transmit sensitive personal data. Any information stored locally on the device is managed using standard operating system protections provided by the device manufacturer.

7. Children's Privacy

WireSmith is not intended specifically for children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If the privacy practices of WireSmith change in the future, this policy will be updated and made available in the app and app store listing.
