About M-PAX

M-PAX Group specializes in developing professional applications for healthcare professionals and educational games for children. Our medical apps, including Log4AS and LogICU, are designed to simplify the documentation process for doctors in training.

With years of experience working closely with medical professionals in the UK and Australia, we understand the unique challenges faced by trainees and consultants. Our solutions are practical, user-friendly, and designed to meet real-world needs.
